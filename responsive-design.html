The 100

The 100 is a post-apocalyptic sci-fi tv series that follows a group of survivors who return to Earth from space to see if the Earth is really survivable.They're like group of prisoners and their punishment is going down to Earth too see if there's any more chemical radiation going on or to check if bad things have evolved down there. When I watch it, I was trying to process all the happenings, it's just too much for my brain. Every episode is so stressful because of new problems being introduced along with characters including the most hated character. I finished it until Season 7 and overall, I would definitely watch it again.

Peaky Blinders

Peaky Blinders is a British crime series set in Birminghan, England during the aftermath of World War I starring Cillian Murphy. It follows the Shelby crime family and they're like gangs trying to rise their power aand navigate the criminal underworld. While watching it, I would say it's difficult to understand the whole things. It's too much for my brain to take since it's a mixture of business, crimes, guns and wars. But when you acknowledge what's going on, youll get hooked to it and you will side with the gang called Peaky Blinders.

The Queen's Gambit

The Queen's Gambit is a drama mini-series based on the novel of Walter Tevis. It follows the story of Beth Harmon, an orphaned chess prodigy as she rises to the top of the male-dominated world competitive chess in the 1960s. I love playing chess that is why it pushed me to watch this wonderful series. I adore how powerful Beth is. While I watching, it ignites the desire within me to play chess again after not doing it for years. I am not that pro-player but this series inspired me to learn and join groups for chess especifically for mybgrowth and for doing the things that I really love.

My Favorite Anime

Here are some of my favorite animes. I love them so much. These mean to me a lot.

Hunter X Hunter

Hunter X Hunter is a Japanese manga-anime series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi. It's a story about Gon Freecs, who aspires to become a Hunter. Along the way, he encounters friends and foes and most thrilling adventures. While watching it, I tried to take it slow down as I would not want to finish it. My favorite characters would definitely be Killua and Kurapika. They are both amazing and they first caught my attention while watching this anime series.

Bungou Stray Dogs

Bungou Stray Dogs is a Japanese seinen manga-anime series written by Kafka Asagiri and illustrated by Sango Harukawa. It's a story of Atsushi Nakajima an orphans who joined the Armed Detective Agency where he met those supernatural detectives including my favorite character and my ultmate crush of all time, Dazai Osamu. Everything about the story is exciting especially when they are all involved in cases of dangerous criminals and characters with mysterious abilities.

Serial Experiments Lain

Serial Experiments Lain is an avant-garde anime series directed by Ryūtarō Nakamura, written by Chiaki J. Konaka, and produced by Yasuyuki Ueda. It is about Lain Iwakura a socially introverted teenager who become curious in digital worlf after receiving messages from a classmate who commited suicicde. When I first watch it, it's really hard to understand what's going on. It's too much for my brain to process all the concepts and the happenings. It's just too deep. But when you're linked to the series, you'll eventually understand it. Also I love the OS here titled "Duvet" by Boa.
